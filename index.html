About Our Startup

We are committed to reducing plastic Flex banner carry bag pollution through innovative recycling solutions.

Flex Modify Solutions Pvt Ltd is a pioneering startup focused on transforming plastic Flex banner carry bag waste into reusable plastic chips. Our mission is to address the global plastic pollution crisis by creating a circular economy for plastics, where waste becomes a valuable resource for manufacturing new products.

With state-of-the-art technology and a dedicated team, we process various types of plastic Flex banner carry bag waste, converting them into high-quality plastic chips that can be used in multiple industries including packaging, construction, and consumer goods.

Collection & Sorting

We collect plastic carry bag waste from various sources and sort it by type and quality to ensure optimal processing.

Cleaning & Shredding

Carry bags are thoroughly cleaned to remove contaminants, then shredded into small pieces for further processing.

Melting & Extrusion

Shredded plastic is melted and extruded to form uniform plastic strands that are cooled and cut into chips.

Quality Control & Packaging

Each batch undergoes rigorous quality testing before being packaged for distribution to manufacturers.

Innovation & Development

We continuously innovate and develop new products and processes to improve recycling efficiency and sustainability.
